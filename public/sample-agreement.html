2. Definitions

“Carbon Credit” means one verified emission reduction or removal unit representing one metric tonne of carbon dioxide equivalent (tCO&sub2;e), registered on a Recognised Registry and eligible for retirement against the Buyer’s climate commitments.
“Delivery” means the confirmed transfer of Carbon Credits from the Seller’s registry account to the Buyer’s registry account, as evidenced by the Registry’s transfer confirmation.
“Recognised Registry” means Verra (VCS), Gold Standard, or the American Carbon Registry (ACR).
“Settlement Date” means the date on which Delivery is confirmed on the Registry.
“Escrow Account” means the segregated client account maintained by CarbonBridge for holding Buyer funds pending Delivery.

4.1 Payment Method

Payment shall be made via bank transfer to the CarbonBridge Escrow Account. Funds must be received within five (5) Business Days of execution of this Agreement. Wire reference must include PA-2026-00142.

4.2 Escrow Arrangement

CarbonBridge shall hold the Total Amount Due in a segregated escrow account. Funds shall be released to the Seller within two (2) Business Days of confirmed Delivery. In the event of non-delivery beyond the Delivery Deadline, CarbonBridge shall refund the Buyer in full, less any applicable administrative fees not exceeding 1% of the Total Amount Due.

5. Delivery and Transfer

5.1 The Seller shall initiate the Registry transfer of the Credits within twenty-four (24) hours of CarbonBridge confirming receipt of cleared funds in escrow.

5.2 Transfer shall be completed within seventy-two (72) hours, subject to Registry processing times (“Delivery Deadline”).

5.3 If the transfer is not completed by the Delivery Deadline, the Buyer may elect to (a) extend the Delivery Deadline by a further 48 hours, or (b) cancel the transaction and receive a full refund from escrow.

5.4 CarbonBridge shall monitor the Registry for transfer confirmation and notify both parties upon Delivery.

6. Insurance

6.1 Non-Delivery Insurance

Underwritten by Kita Earth Ltd. In the event the Seller fails to deliver the Credits by the Delivery Deadline and any extensions, the Buyer shall receive either (a) replacement credits of equivalent type and quality sourced by CarbonBridge, or (b) a full refund of the credit cost plus the insurance premium.

6.2 Invalidation Insurance

Underwritten by CFC Underwriting Ltd (Lloyd’s Syndicate). If the Credits are invalidated by the Registry after Delivery due to methodology failure, reversal, or project cancellation, the Buyer shall receive replacement credits or monetary compensation up to the original purchase price. Coverage period: 36 months from Delivery Date.

7. Title and Risk

7.1 Title to the Credits shall pass from the Seller to the Buyer upon confirmed Delivery on the relevant Registry.

7.2 Risk of loss transfers upon Delivery. Prior to Delivery, the Seller bears all risk of credit invalidation, reversal, or registry failure.
